# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to creating an open, welcoming, and professional community.

---

## Expected Behavior

Be respectful.

Be collaborative.

Provide constructive feedback.

Support new contributors.

Use inclusive language.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment

Spam

Discrimination

Personal attacks

Publishing private information

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers reserve the right to moderate discussions and contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
